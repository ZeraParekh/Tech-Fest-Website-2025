Importance of Trees

Trees are the lungs of our land

Why Trees Matter?

Trees are essential for life on Earth.

They give us oxygen, food, shade, and help fight climate change.

Benefits

Purify the air
Provide shelter for animals
Prevent soil erosion
Keep our planet cool

What Can You Do?

Plant more trees
Take care of existing trees
Spread awareness

Why do plants need only a specific amount of water?

It enables fundamental processes like photosynthesis and transpiration. Not only that but it supports nutrient uptake and prevents stress from both drought and over-saturation.
