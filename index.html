Importance of Trees

Trees are the lungs of our land

Why Trees Matter?

Trees are essential for life on Earth.

They give us oxygen, food, shade, and help fight climate change.

Benefits

  • Purify the air
  • Provide shelter for animals
  • Prevent soil erosion
  • Keep our planet cool
